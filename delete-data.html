Data Deletion Instructions

Because LifeRemind stores 100% of your data locally on your device, deleting your data is instantaneous and permanent.

How to delete your data:

  1. Open the LifeRemind App.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and select "Clear App Data" (if available) OR simply uninstall the app from your device.

Once uninstalled, all local databases are permanently destroyed by your device's operating system.
